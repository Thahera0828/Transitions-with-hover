Iconic KL Rahul Moments
KL Rahul: Test Debut Century vs Australia (Sydney, 2015) KL Rahul announced himself with a classy 110 against Australia in just his second Test.He showed immense composure under pressure at the SCG.It marked the beginning of his journey as a dependable top-order batsman.
KL Rahul: Fastest IPL Fifty (14 balls, 2018) Playing for Kings XI Punjab, Rahul smashed a fiery 50 off just 14 balls against Delhi Daredevils.His explosive knock stunned everyone and set the tone for the season.It remains one of the fastest and most entertaining fifties in IPL history.
KL Rahul: Century on ODI Debut (vs Zimbabwe, 2016) Rahul scored an unbeaten 100* on his ODI debut — the first Indian to achieve this feat.He paced his innings beautifully and guided India to a comfortable win.It proved his adaptabiliy across formats early in his career.
KL Rahul: 129 vs England at Lord’s (2021) Rahul played a majestic 129 at the Home of Cricket, earning a place on the Lord’s Honours Board.He batted with elegance and patience, giving India a vital first-innings lead.This knock was hailed as one of his finest Test innings overseas.
KL Rahul: Match-winning 97 vs Pakistan (Asia Cup 2023) Returning from injury, Rahul scored a calm and classy 97* in a high-pressure clash.He anchored India’s innings and rebuilt brilliantly with Kohli.The knock proved his resilience and ability to perform on big stages.
KL Rahul: 199 vs England (Chennai, 2016) Rahul came agonizingly close to a double century, scoring a magnificent 199.He displayed flawless technique and temperament on a tricky pitch.Though he missed out on 200, it remains one of his most elegant Test knocks.
KL Rahul: 112 vs South Africa (Centurion, 2021)*Opening the innings in tough South African conditions, Rahul scored a brilliant 112*.He stood tall against pace and bounce, guiding India to a strong position.This innings proved his class in overseas Tests.
KL Rahul: 91 vs Royal Challengers Bangalore (IPL 2020) As captain of Kings XI Punjab, Rahul smashed an unbeaten 91* to chase down 206.His timing, calmness, and leadership stood out in a high-scoring match.It was one of his best IPL captain’s knocks — pure dominance and poise.
